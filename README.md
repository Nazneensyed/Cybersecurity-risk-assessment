Blue Mist Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

This project is about doing a cybersecurity risk assessment for a coffee shop called **Blue Mist**. We want to find out what the coffee shop has that is important what kinds of threats it might face and where it is vulnerable. We will use an approach to figure out what the risks are.

## 📌 Overview
The main goal of this project is to show how basic cybersecurity ideas work in a business. We will look at a coffee shop and use simple risk management ideas to keep it safe.

## 🛡️ Key Areas Covered

- We will make a list of what's important to **Blue Mist** like the computers that take orders, laptops and customer information.

- We will think about what kinds of threats **Blue Mist** might face, like emails, bad software and people trying to get in without permission.

- We will look for weaknesses like to-guess passwords and old computer systems.

- We will decide how likely it is that something bad will happen and how bad it would be if it did.

- We will come up with plans to make **Blue Mist** like using special codes to log in protecting the computer network and teaching employees about cybersecurity.

## 📊 Contents

- I have a report on the risk assessment that you can download as a PDF file.

- I have a table that shows all the risks and what we thought about them.

- I explain cybersecurity ideas in a way that is easy to understand even if you are new to this.

## 🎯 Purpose

I made this project to help people learn about cybersecurity risk assessment. It is a way to get started and to show that you understand the basics, which is important for entry-level jobs, in cybersecurity.

## 📚 References

- I used the NIST Risk Management Framework to guide our work.

- I also looked at the ISO/IEC 27001 standards.

-. I considered the OWASP Top 10 list of common cybersecurity problems.
